COMMERCE. ·   MENA-INSPIRED BEVERAGES WHO WE ARE Where Culture Meets Commerce Parallel Foods, INC. is a food and beverage holding company built on the belief that the richest flavors come from the most storied places. We create, incubate, and grow brands that bring the depth of global culture — particularly the vibrant heritage of the MENA region — to the shelves of a new generation. Our brands are not products. They are portals. Each one is an invitation to experience a world through taste — where the ancient marketplace meets the modern consumer, and where tradition is not preserved but reimagined. Parallel Foods operates at the intersection of authentic cultural identity and contemporary brand building. We are committed to quality formulation, responsible sourcing, and storytelling that honors the communities that inspire us. FLAGSHIP BRAND AL SOUQ Drinks MENA-inspired canned teas crafted for Gen Z.
